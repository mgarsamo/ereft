‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties?

Use the search bar on the home screen or tap the filter icon to set specific criteria like location, price range, property type, and number of bedrooms.

Can I save properties for later?

Yes! Tap the heart icon on any property to add it to your favorites. Access your saved properties from the Favorites tab.

How do I contact property owners?

Tap on any property to view details, then use the contact information provided to reach the property owner directly.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Ereft is completely free for property browsing and searching. Property owners can list their properties with our simple listing process.

Which cities are covered?

Ereft covers major Ethiopian cities including Addis Ababa, Dire Dawa, Bahir Dar, Hawassa, Mekelle, and many more.
